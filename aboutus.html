About Us

Welcome to EV Ireland, your premier destination for all things electric vehicles (EVs) in Ireland! Founded by a passionate college student entering his second year, Michael Gleeson, this platform is a testament to his love for coding in HTML and CSS, sprinkled with a dash of Bootstrap brilliance.

Michael Gleeson embarked on this electrifying journey to combine his technological prowess with his deep appreciation for sustainable transportation. Fueled by a desire to make a positive impact on the environment, he has meticulously crafted a high-quality website that serves as a hub for EV enthusiasts and eco-conscious individuals alike.

Our website is a reflection of Michael Gleeson's dedication to clean energy and innovation. Through a seamless blend of HTML and CSS, with Bootstrap providing a touch of modernity, he has created an aesthetically pleasing and user-friendly space. Navigating through the site, users will discover a wealth of information on the latest EV models, charging infrastructure updates, government incentives, and everything else related to the electric vehicle landscape in Ireland.

EV Ireland stands out not only for its visually appealing design but also for its commitment to delivering accurate and up-to-date content. Michael Gleeson understands the importance of staying current in the ever-evolving world of electric vehicles, and he ensures that the website is a reliable source for enthusiasts and newcomers alike.

Join us on this exciting journey towards a greener future as we explore the world of electric vehicles in Ireland together. Whether you're a seasoned EV driver, a curious college student, or someone considering making the switch to sustainable transportation, EV Ireland is here to inform, inspire, and connect like-minded individuals. Welcome to the electrifying experience of EV Ireland, where passion meets technology for a cleaner, brighter tomorrow.
